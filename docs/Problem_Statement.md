# Problem Statement

Digital banking users frequently rely on account recovery processes when they forget passwords, lose devices, or need to update contact information.

Fraudsters exploit these recovery workflows through social engineering, stolen credentials, fake identities, and compromised devices to gain unauthorized access to customer accounts.

Banks require a secure, scalable, and privacy-preserving mechanism to detect suspicious account recovery attempts without creating unnecessary friction for legitimate users.

The challenge is to develop an intelligent system that can identify high-risk recovery requests in real time and trigger additional verification only when required.
